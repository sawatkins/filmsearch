Welcome! FilmSearch is a AI search engine to discover movies using natural language. I created it to simplify movie discovery, uncover hidden gems, and find films matching specific moods or interests.

It uses OpenAI's GPT-4o model to understand natural language queries and find the most relevant movies. Then it uses the TMDB API to get movie posters.

Contact & Feedback

For feedback, suggestions, or other questions, feel free to email me.

Privacy

I don't collect personal information, store cookies, or use tracking. I log search queries to understand and improve the results (and beacuse I'm curious what people search for).

Keep in mind that search queries get sent to OpenAI's API for processing, so refer to their privacy policy.
